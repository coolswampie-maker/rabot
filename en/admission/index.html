For applicants

Admission

Everything you need to apply to RUDN MBA programs: the admission steps, the document checklist, the entrance essay topics and downloadable forms.

1

Submit an enquiry

Contact the admissions team — we will tell you about the programs and format and guide you through admission.

2

Prepare your documents

Gather the document package (see the list below) and fill in the application and the data-processing consent.

3

Write the essays

Prepare the motivation essays on the entrance-examination topics.

4

Interview and enrolment

Have an interview with the admissions committee and sign the study agreement.

What to prepare

Documents for admission

Russian citizens

  1. 1Applicant’s application form
  2. 2Higher-education diploma with its supplement
  3. 3Copy of the work record book / certificate from the employer confirming at least 2 years of experience, certified by HR
  4. 42 photographs 3×4 cm (electronic format for online submission)
  5. 5Copy of the passport pages (full name, passport number and registration)
  6. 6Copy of SNILS
  7. 7Motivation essay
  8. 8Consent to the processing of personal data

Foreign citizens

  1. 1Applicant’s application form
  2. 2Higher-education diploma with its supplement
  3. 3Notarised copies of the diploma and its supplement in Russian/English
  4. 4Apostille
  5. 5Copy of the work record book / certificate from the employer confirming at least 2 years of experience, certified by HR
  6. 62 photographs 3×4 cm (electronic format for online submission)
  7. 7Copy of the passport pages (full name, passport number and registration)
  8. 8Visa
  9. 9Motivation essay
  10. 10Completed consent to the processing of passport data

Entrance examination

Essay topics

1

Provide an objective description of yourself, emphasising the personal qualities — your strengths and weaknesses — that have influenced your development. You may support your story with examples.

600 words

2

Describe two of your most significant achievements (if possible, one personal and one professional). Explain your choice.

400 words

3

Describe a situation where you experienced a failure (in your personal or professional life). What lesson did you learn from it?

400 words

4

A) Tell us about your short-term and long-term career plans (300 words). B) How will studying in the MBA program help you achieve your goals? (250 words).

300 + 250 words

5

Optional. Is there anything you did not mention in your previous essays that you would like to share with the admissions committee?

350 words
