ICEMR

ICEMR — International Center for Emerging Markets Research

The International Center for Emerging Markets Research (ICEMR) conducts policy and technical analysis that arises from emerging markets and their continuous need to support and foster rapid growth, implement constant innovation, and spur competitiveness. We aim to equip researchers, stakeholders and decision-makers with effective frameworks and valuable research to better understand the impact and role of their decisions in fostering growth in emerging markets.
